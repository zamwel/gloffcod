Global Foundation for Community Development Sustainability Leadership Summit

The Executive Board of Global Foundation For Community Development in Collaboration with NUGS UDS is pleased to to invite applications for its upcoming Sustainability Leadership Training to be held at the University of Development Studies Tamale-Ghana at on the 5th of of July 2022 at 10:00am GMT. The education seeks to expose the youth to the Sustainable Development Goals and make them ambassadors for positive change in the society based on the theme Developing The Right Attitude And Mindset To Become Ambassadors for the Sustainable Development Goals.

Global Foundation for Community Development is a non profit organization championing the sustainable development goals through implementation of sustainable projects and programs in Ghana. Our mission is to establish flourishing communities devoid of poverty, all forms of injustices and racial discrimination. Our four main strategic objectives are to provide safe water to communties in need of it, to train and empower the youth, women empowerment and climate action and has so far trained over 200 people who are making impacts in their various communities.

Background: Guided by the firm believe that when the youths are empowered, they will be able to take bold actions to address the worlds pressing needs towards the attainment of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals we deem it important to embark on this training.
Date: 05/02/2022 Time: 10:00am -1:30pm Venue:University of Development Studies Tamale Multi Purpose Auditorium.

Topics:

  • Unlocking the strategies to start impactful initiatives
  • Sustainability and me
  • The role of the youth in tackling climate change
  • Hunessing Opportunities through business innovation.
  • Time Management
